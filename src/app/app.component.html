Welcome! This website is meant to be used alongside Legends of Runeterra. We are keeping track of win streaks for decks.

 

The 'Local Player Stats' tab makes calls to your local Legends of Runeterra game client to pick up your current deck and game results. If you don't have a client open, mock data will populate instead.

 

The 'Leaderboard' tab displays information about players with the highest win streaks regardless of decks. You are able to click the profile icon on the leaderboards or enter in a name to search for records related to that player. Enter in a player name from the end of the LCS summer split to receive records.
